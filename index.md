# Hi — I’m **Boda Huo**

Master’s student in Mechanical Engineering at **Carnegie Mellon University** (’26). I work at the intersection of **robot learning (RL)**, **visuo-tactile sensing**, and **whole-body control** for humanoids and legged systems.

- **Current:** Safe AI Lab (CMU) — building a robust **visuo-tactile** sensing stack and policy pipelines.
- **Previously:** Hawkes Lab (UCSB) — **soft vine-robot** anchoring mechanisms for Mars-regolith analogs.

**Interests:** robot learning for loco-manipulation, tactile + vision fusion, sim-to-real, adaptive control (LQI/MRAC).

**Contact:** bodah@andrew.cmu.edu · [GitHub](https://github.com/bodah-silv) · [Website](https://bodah-silv.github.io)
